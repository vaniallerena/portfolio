About Me

Since I was a young girl, I have always had a big interest in working with graphics, typography, and colors. I also used to collect brochures, flyers, and magazines, and spend minutes looking at their designs. Because of my big interest in graphic design, I decided to follow a career path about my passion to improve my design skills, become a professional in what I love the most, and contribute to my community. I am looking for opportunities to train my skills and showcase my talent. I can provide a fresh and unique approach to design solutions, and I am always willing to discuss my ideas with others. I can also bring a different cultural perspective to work. College projects like designing brochures, posters, business cards, logos, and social media posts help me to polish my design skills, learn to critique my own work, and give feedback to my peers. In the future, I would like to work with non-profit organizations, design agencies, or small businesses.
